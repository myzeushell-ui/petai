## What does your company do?

PetAI is a vertical AI marketplace for the pet industry — a single ecosystem that follows the owner's entire journey from "which dog should I get" to "my dog had puppies and I sold them on the same app." Seven integrated modules: free AI breed quiz (top-of-funnel), health records with AI lab analysis, marketplace with verified breeders, AI assistant trained on each pet's history, smart collar with voice AI (bark emotion classification), breeding suite with COI calculator and auto-generated contracts, and online vet consultations.

The data moat compounds: more pets → smarter voice AI → better health predictions → higher retention.

---

## Why now?

1. **Voice AI maturity:** 2024 Earth Species Project + Wav2Vec2 hit 87% accuracy on dog vocalizations (Cornell 2025) — finally usable for consumer products.
2. **Pet-care market growth:** US $147B in 2025, +6.7% YoY (APPA 2025). Mid-market still un-digitized: 67% of owners miss vaccines or doses.
3. **AI labs becoming commodity:** Multimodal LLMs (Claude 3.5, GPT-4o) can now read lab PDFs and translate medical jargon — a non-starter 18 months ago.
4. **iPhone-replacement timing for pet wearables:** Whistle/Fi sold ~3M units, but stay single-purpose. Owners want one app, not five.

## Standard questions answers (ready-to-paste)

### "What's the most important thing about your company?"
Ecosystem lock-in. We're the only player covering the entire pet ownership lifecycle in one app. Free AI breed quiz drives organic acquisition; marketplace monetizes purchase intent; daily health-tracking creates retention; smart collar builds proprietary voice data the moat compounds on.

### "What do you understand about your market that others don't?"
Pet owners 25–45 already use Apple Health / Whoop / Strava. They expect the same data-driven, AI-explained tracking for their pets — but every existing pet app is either a dumb GPS tracker, a yellow-pages of vets, or a banal photo-sharing toy. Nobody has built the "operating system." The market is ready; the product wasn't.

### "What's your biggest risk?"
Hardware. Smart collar production at <$50 BOM with reliable bark classification needs serious manufacturing partnership (likely China). We'll launch software-only first, ship hardware in Phase 3 once retention proves out.

### "Who are your competitors?"
**Pet wearables:** Whistle, Fi (GPS only, no AI, no marketplace).
**Pet-care apps:** PetDesk, FirstVet (vet booking only, no health data).
**Marketplaces:** Avito, Craigslist pet sections (zero quality control, no AI).
**No one is doing all of these in one ecosystem.**

### "How will you make money?"
- Subscriptions: Free / Pro $4.99/mo / Breeder $9.99/mo / Clinic B2B from $29.99/mo
- Transactional: 20% on vet consultations · 3% marketplace · 5% on breeding deals · $4.90 fixed for auto-generated breeding contracts
- Hardware (Phase 3): smart collar $129 one-time
- LTV projection: $180 for Pro consumer, $420 for Breeder, $2,400 for Clinic B2B
