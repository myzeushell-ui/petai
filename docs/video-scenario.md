# PetAI — "My Dog Journey"

⏱ 80–90 sec · 8 scenes

---

## SCENE 1 — THE WALK
**📺 Live footage:** dogs, people, you and your brother

> I wanted a dog. But which one? There are hundreds of breeds. Which one fits my apartment, my schedule, my family?

---

## SCENE 2 — FINDING THE RIGHT ONE
**📺 Screen:** quiz → results → marketplace

> I downloaded PetAI. Took a quiz. The app picked the right breed for my lifestyle. Found a verified breeder right in the marketplace. Got my puppy.

---

## SCENE 3 — HEALTH CARD AND NUTRITION
**📺 Screen:** dashboard → health score → nutrition

> Right away — a health card. Vaccines, blood tests, weight — all in one place. AI built a balanced diet plan — for the breed, age, weight and activity. What to feed, how much, when.

---

## SCENE 4 — THE PROBLEM
**📺 Screen:** AI chat → consultation → payment

> My dog had a behavior problem. I found a dog trainer in the app. Booked a session. Paid. Got a training plan. All inside PetAI.

---

## SCENE 5 — BECOMING A BREEDER
**📺 Screen:** breeding → heat tracker → COI → match

> Time passed. I decided to breed. Heat cycle tracker. Mating match by pedigree. Inbreeding calculator. Breeding contract — generated automatically.

---

## SCENE 6 — SELLING PUPPIES
**📺 Screen:** marketplace → create listing

> Puppies were born. I listed them on PetAI marketplace. Pedigree, documents, photos. Buyers find them, message me, buy — all in one app.

---

## SCENE 7 — SMART COLLAR
**📺 Screen:** voice AI → waveform → emotions + live dog footage

> And then — the smart collar. Temperature, movement, heart rate. And a microphone that listens to barks and understands how your dog feels — stress, joy, pain, anxiety. You know before you even notice.

---

## SCENE 8 — FINALE
**📺 Live dog footage → your face to camera**

> One app. From "which dog should I get" — to breeding and selling puppies. PetAI.

---

## THE JOURNEY

- Want a dog → breed quiz
- Found one → marketplace
- Got one → health card + nutrition
- Problem → trainer + payment
- Breeding → heat + mating + contract
- Puppies → sell on marketplace
- Every day → collar knows how she feels
