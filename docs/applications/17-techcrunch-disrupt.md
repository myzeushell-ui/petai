**Step 5: Why is this disruptive?**
```
Pet care industry — $147B US alone — is structurally over-supplied with
products and under-supplied with information. Owners can buy 50 brands
of dog food but can't decode why their dog limped this morning.

PetAI disrupts the FOUR existing categories simultaneously:
1. Telehealth pet platforms (Pawp, Vetster): we are the BEFORE/AFTER
   layer, not the consultation itself
2. Smart collars (FitBark, Tractive, Whistle): we sit ABOVE their data,
   making their hardware 10x more valuable to owners
3. Pet retail apps (Chewy, PetSmart): we are the BREED INTELLIGENCE
   layer that drives smarter purchases
4. Veterinary practice management (Banfield): we are the OWNER INTERFACE
   that makes vet visits more productive

No competitor combines all four. By going horizontal across these
verticals, PetAI becomes the default "first app" pet owners check —
the WhatsApp of pet care information.
```
