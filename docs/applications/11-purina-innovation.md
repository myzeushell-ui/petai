**Step 3: Why does this matter for pet care?**
```
Pet owners face a fundamental information asymmetry — they spend $147B/year
treating pets like family, but receive veterinary guidance designed for
medical professionals. The owner is the most under-equipped stakeholder
in the most over-funded industry.

PetAI changes this by giving every owner an AI second opinion in plain
English — for bark behavior, lab results, breed-specific lifecycle care,
and trusted-provider discovery. This isn't replacing vets — it's helping
owners arrive at the vet with the right questions, the right context,
and the right urgency.

For Purina specifically: PetAI users represent the highest-intent
pet-food buyers (millennials/Gen-Z who treat pets as family members).
Integration with Purina nutrition recommendations inside our /nutrition
tab would be a natural fit — and our 20-breed lifecycle care timeline
already includes nutrition recommendations by life stage.
```
