## ⚠️ ОСОБЫЕ МОМЕНТЫ

### AppSumo Originals vs Plus
- **Originals** — featured launch на AppSumo homepage, 30-day campaign with email blasts, +20-50K potential reach. Selectivity high (10-20% acceptance).
- **Plus** — лонг-tail listing без featured campaign. Меньше revenue, проще acceptance.

**Применять на Originals — стоит того, если PetAI готов handle большой initial spike трафика.**

### Lifetime deal downside
- Users pay once → use forever → eats future subscription revenue
- Стандартное "противоядие" — cap total LTD codes на 2000 max, потом switch на subscription only
- Это пере-инвестиция в **runway extension** vs long-term subscription LTV. Для pre-seed это часто acceptable trade-off

### Revenue split
- **AppSumo takes 30%** of gross LTD revenue (включая any tier upgrades within 60 days)
- **Net to PetAI:** 70% of $25-200K gross = $17.5K-140K cash
- **Payout schedule:** AppSumo paid out 30-60 days after campaign close
