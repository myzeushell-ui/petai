### Problem statement
```
Pet owners spend $147B/year in the US treating their pets like family,
but the industry still treats them like medical novices. Lab results are
cryptic. Breed advice is generic. Behavioral signals (why is my dog
limping / barking / scratching?) get dismissed. The owner is the most
under-equipped stakeholder in the most over-funded industry.
```

### Solution
```
PetAI is the AI translation layer between pet and vet — voice AI for
behavior, lab interpretation, breed-specific lifecycle care, and a
marketplace of vetted providers. All in one app, bilingual EN/RU.
```

### Why now?
```
Three converging shifts: (1) pet humanization peaked (post-COVID lock-in),
(2) AI foundation models now interpret unstructured data at consumer-grade
accuracy, (3) smart collar hardware fragmentation needs a unifying OS
layer. PetAI is positioned as that layer.
```
