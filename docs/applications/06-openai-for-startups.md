### Why OpenAI specifically?
```
While our primary AI infrastructure currently uses Claude (Anthropic),
we want to integrate GPT models for:
1. A/B testing user satisfaction on identical pet health questions
   between Claude and GPT — multi-model strategy reduces single-vendor risk
2. Function calling for marketplace search ("find me a vet within 5 miles
   that specializes in golden retrievers") — GPT-4o has best function
   calling for our consumer flow
3. DALL-E 3 for generating breed-specific care illustrations in our
   lifecycle upbringing system
4. Whisper for voice transcription of owner descriptions of pet symptoms
```
