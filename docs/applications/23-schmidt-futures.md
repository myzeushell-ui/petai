**Why this matters at scale**
```
PetAI sits at the intersection of three societal trends Schmidt Futures
typically supports:

1. AI democratization: PetAI proves consumer-grade pet healthcare AI is
   ready for mass deployment. The methodology — multimodal pet health
   interpretation — translates to other underserved domains (elder
   care, child development).

2. Health information equity: pet care is among the most stratified
   information markets — wealthy owners access specialty vets,
   middle-class owners get incomplete advice from 5-min generic vet
   visits. PetAI democratizes interpretation, with proven methods that
   scale to human health analogues.

3. Cross-language data scarcity: bark-AI models are typically English-
   trained on US dog vocalizations. PetAI's bilingual data collection
   (EN + RU pet behavioral data) builds the world's first cross-cultural
   pet vocalization training set — usable as research artifact beyond
   our product.
```

Outcome targets in 12 months:
