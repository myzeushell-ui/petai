**Problem statement (200 слов)**
```
The American pet care market is $147B+/year, with $50B+ on pet health
alone. Yet pet owners — who treat their pets as family members — receive
veterinary guidance designed for medical professionals.

Three critical gaps PetSmart customers face daily:
1. Lab result confusion: vets hand owners a PDF of blood test results
   they can't interpret. Result: $200 follow-up visits to ask basic
   questions.
2. Behavioral signal dismissal: owner notices dog is anxious, barking
   differently, eating less — vet says "monitor and bring back if worse"
   because no quantifiable data exists. Owner anxiety + delayed
   treatment.
3. Breed-specific care fragmentation: owners must research 20 different
   blog posts to understand what's normal for their breed at each life
   stage. No unified guidance source.

PetAI solves all three with an AI second opinion in plain English:
bark-analysis voice AI, lab interpretation, lifecycle care timeline for
20+ breeds. Sits above the vet experience, not replacing it.

PetSmart customers — particularly Banfield Pet Hospital users — would
benefit from PetAI between their store/vet visits as the daily care
companion.
```

**Solution description**
Используй **medium description (150 слов)** из master-kit.

**Why PetSmart specifically?**
```
PetSmart's 2000+ stores + Banfield Pet Hospital network is the ideal
distribution channel for PetAI. Specific integration opportunities:
1. In-store QR codes: customers scan → land on PetAI breed-specific
   shopping list for their pet (food, supplements, toys), cross-sells
   PetSmart inventory
2. Banfield integration: lab results from Banfield visits auto-populate
   PetAI for plain-English interpretation between vet visits
3. Pet adoption events: PetSmart Charities adopters get free 3-month
   PetAI Premium, increasing first-year retention and reducing returns
4. Cura program specifically: PetSmart commits to 100-store pilot of
   PetAI in-store activation, measuring lift in associated product
   sales (food, supplements, toys recommended by PetAI for that pet's
   breed and life stage)
```

**Funding ask**
```
$50K Cura cash + 100-store pilot commitment. Use of funds: 3 months
of paid acquisition in pilot store markets to measure foot-traffic-
to-app-install conversion. Validates the in-store distribution
hypothesis.
```
