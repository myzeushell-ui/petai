```
PetAI is the first AI app combining four pet-care intelligence layers in
one product:
1. Bark-analysis voice AI — classifying dog emotional state from audio
2. Lab result AI interpretation in plain English
3. Lifecycle care timeline for 20+ dog breeds
4. Vetted vet/trainer/breeder marketplace
```

```
behavior, lab results, or breed-specific care. PetAI bridges that gap,
making the owner-to-professional interaction more productive (fewer
unnecessary visits, faster diagnoses, better compliance with care plans).
```
